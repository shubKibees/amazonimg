Dashboard
overview

The image provided below offers a detailed view of our comprehensive dashboard. It's designed to be user-friendly, presenting you with all the essential information at a glance.

Admin

overview

The admin section contains tabs for Users, Roles, Permissions, Menu, and Operations. Each tab has its respective page showing what it represents.

Pages

overview

When you interact with the page tab on the website by clicking it, a comprehensive list of all the pages that are currently available on the website will be displayed for you.

Banners

overview

When you proceed to the 'Banners' tab, you will be able to view an extensive list of all the banners that are currently displayed on the website. In addition to this, you'll find an 'Action' tab. This particular feature provides you with the ability to make modifications to these banners. Whether you want to change the content, edit the design, or completely overhaul a banner, the 'Action' tab offers you the flexibility and control to do so.

  • for Edit : Action —> Edit
  • for View : Action —> Show
  • for Delete: Action —> Delete

Product

overview

The Product tab on our website is crucial for navigating through our diverse product offerings. This tab is thoughtfully divided into two main sections: the Product Category and the Product List.

The Product Category section is designed to help you quickly identify and navigate through the different categories of products available. It presents a comprehensive list of all product categories. This allows you to easily find and select the category that you are interested in, making your shopping experience streamlined and efficient.

On the other hand, the Product List section provides a detailed view of all the products available within each category.

Media (Blog)

overview

The media(blog) section will provide you with a comprehensive list of all blogs that have been published on the website. Each listed blog comes with an accompanying 'action' tab. This tab is a practical tool that allows you to perform various tasks related to the management of the blog posts. You can use it to edit the content of the blog if updates or corrections are necessary. The 'show' option lets you view the blog as it appears on the website, which is useful for checking the final layout before it is viewed by the public. Lastly, the 'delete' function is available if you decide that a particular blog post should be removed from the website.

  • for Edit : Action —> Edit
  • for View : Action —> Show
  • for Delete: Action —> Delete

Partners

overview

The "Partners" tab, a feature on our admin, plays a crucial role in managing our partnerships. Once accessed, this tab will exhibit a comprehensive list of all our partners who have registered on the website.

In addition to displaying the registered partners, the "Partners" tab also provides admittance to a list in the administrative section of the website.

you can also edit the partners

  • for Edit : Action —> Edit
  • for View : Action —> Show
  • for Delete: Action —> Delete

Form Data

overview

The data collected from the form on the website provides comprehensive details about the individuals who filled out the enquiry form. These details include, but are not limited to, the person's name, their contact phone number, their email address, and other relevant information.

Settings

overview

From the settings panel, you are given the ability to modify the contact information that is displayed on the website. This includes not only the primary contact details, but also the links to various social media platforms. These social media links are represented by their respective icons, and by clicking on these icons, users can directly access your social media profiles.
